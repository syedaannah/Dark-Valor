About the Tournament

Battle for the Eternal Throne

The Dark Valor: Arena Grand Tournament is the most prestigious fighting competition in the realm. Warriors from every faction converge to claim glory and the coveted Eternal Throne.

Each fighter brings a unique style — from the armored might of the Legion to the shadow-infused powers of the Heralds. Six elite warriors enter. Only one leaves as champion.

Season IV features 6 elite warriors competing across three brutal rounds. Every fight pushes limits. Every victory shapes destiny.
